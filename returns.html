Shipping Info

MSG is a small team and personally fulfills all orders placed on a first come first serve basis. Please allow 3-5 days for your order to be processed. We ship via USPS Meida Mail in the United States. If you have not received your item after 20 days from the date of purchase, please contact us. We appreciate your patience.

International Orders

If you wish to make an international order, please email us separately at msgflavor@gmail.com so we can give you a shipping estimate. Please note that international shipping may be costly and slow depending on your country.

Local Pick-Up

Local Pickup is available Mon-Fri from 10am – 10pm in Bushwick, NY in Brooklyn. Please email us at msgflavor@gmail.com beforehand to arrange a time with us.

Returns

Unfortunately we are unable to process returns at this time. However, we value you, and will do everything in our power to make sure you're satisfied with your purchase. If you have an issue with your order, please contact us at msgflavor@gmail.com.

Care Information

Some elements on our items are hand-stitched, hand-printed, and/or hand-assembled. For best care practices, please wash hand-wash all garments inside-out with cold/warm water. Air dry.
